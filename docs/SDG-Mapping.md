# Salma Unity Care Hospital — SDG & Impact Mapping

This document maps Salma Unity Care Hospital modules to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), showing how the hospital’s blockchain‑integrated services produce measurable civic impact.

---

## SDG Summary Table

| SDG  | Focus                                | Hospital Module Alignment                          | Primary Impact                       |
|------|--------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| SDG 3 | Good Health & Well‑being             | Patient identity, encrypted records, clinical workflows | Safer care, continuity, outcomes     |

---

## SDG 3 — Good Health & Well‑being
- **Patient identity & access:** Verifiable credentials for patients, clinicians, and staff; role‑based access improves safety.  
- **Encrypted medical records:** End‑to‑end encryption, consent logs, and immutable audit trails strengthen continuity of care.  
- **Clinical workflows:** Time‑stamped care pathways, lab/radiology attachments, and discharge summaries improve outcomes.  

---

## SDG 9 — Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure
- **Health data rails:** Standardized data models and APIs support interoperability across hospitals and insurers.  
- **Payments infrastructure:** ISO 20022‑aligned rails for billing, claims, and settlements reduce friction.  
- **High availability:** Redundant nodes, secure key management, and monitored uptime create resilience.  

---

## SDG 16 — Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions
- **Consent governance:** Explicit consent capture, revocation trails, and role audits enhance compliance.  
- **Compliance engine:** Policy enforcement and automated reporting align with regulators.  
- **Tamper evidence:** Immutable logs deter fraud and strengthen accountability.  

---

## SDG 17 — Partnerships for the Goals
- **Insurer & bank integrations:** Claims processing, settlements, and financing support collaboration.  
- **Public health data sharing:** Aggregated, privacy‑preserving reporting for ministries and research partners.  
- **Standards alignment:** Interoperability profiles enable cross‑border cooperation.  

---

## SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities
- **Inclusive onboarding:** Low‑friction digital identity and remote access reduce barriers.  
- **Subsidy & benefit routing:** Verified eligibility flows support equitable care.  
- **Accessibility:** Multichannel communication and inclusive UX improve reach.  

---

## SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities & Communities
- **Emergency coordination:** Secure dispatch, triage, and capacity signals support citywide response.  
- **Environmental health links:** Air/water quality feeds inform preventive care.  
- **Community health programs:** Verified outreach and outcomes tracking improve services.  

---

## SDG 8 — Decent Work & Economic Growth
- **Workforce credentials:** Verified licensing, training, and shift logs protect patients and staff.  
- **Operational efficiency:** Automated claims, procurement, and inventory reduce waste.  
- **Fair compensation rails:** Transparent payroll and benefits improve retention.  

---

## SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption & Production
- **Supply chain tracking:** Lot/batch tracking reduces counterfeits and waste.  
- **Pharmacy controls:** Dispense audits and interaction checks enhance safety.  
- **Materials reporting:** Sustainability metrics support responsible sourcing.  

---

## Measurement & Audit
- **Impact KPIs:** Access equity, readmission rates, claims cycle time, stockout frequency, audit findings.  
- **Data quality:** Completeness, timeliness, and accuracy checks with automated alerts.  
- **Independent review:** Exportable reports and verifier roles for regulators and partners.
